# Dynamic-Wireless-Power-Transfer-System
Energize EV presents a self-sustainable EV charging system integrating solar, wind, and smart grid technologies with real-time power allocation. Resonant inductive coupling enables wireless on-the-go charging. IoT monitoring ensures reliability, addressing infrastructure limitations and promoting green, scalable electric mobility.
Energize EV introduces a self-sustainable power system aimed at revolutionizing
electric vehicle (EV) charging through dynamic wireless energy transfer and
efficient power allocation. The system integrates renewable energy sources such
as solar and wind with smart grid technologies to create a sustainable energy
ecosystem. Real-time power allocation algorithms optimize energy distribution
based on EV demand, grid load, and renewable energy availability, ensuring
efficient and balanced energy utilization.
Wireless energy transfer, implemented through resonant inductive coupling,
facilitates on- the-go charging, eliminating the need for physical charging stations
and reducing downtime.The system is equipped with IoT-enabled monitoring and
control features for enhanced reliability and adaptability to varying energy needs.
This project seeks to address critical challenges in EV adoption, such as charging
infrastructure limitations and reliance on non-renewable energy, while promoting
environmental sustainability. By combining innovation with practicality, Energize
EV aims to pave the way for a scalable and efficient future in electric mobility.
